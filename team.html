Mr. Mahendra R. Sangoi

Founder & Chairman, RamDev Group

I have seen Ramdev Group growing immensely by leaps and bounds over the years. From the humble beginnings of concrete and pavers block manufacturing in 1998, we have steadily expanded into real estate, industrial leasing, and entertainment.

This journey is not mine alone — it’s built with the effort of great minds, dedicated partners, and a strong team. Over the past 30 years, I’ve had the privilege to work with exceptional individuals, bringing our shared vision to life.

The future most definitely seems promising. I aim to sustain our growth through innovation, new technology, and fresh ideas.
