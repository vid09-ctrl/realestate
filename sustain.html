CSR – Sustaining the Society that Sustains Us

Ramdev Group believes in inclusivity. We mean equal benefits for all sections of society. From animal welfare to tribal education and medical support, we work to serve.

Animal Welfare

Mr. Mahendra Sangoi, founder of Ramdev Group, is a strong believer in animal rights. He is the managing trustee of Bhagwan Mahavir Pashu Raksha Kendra – India’s largest veterinary hospital with 100+ beds for animals. The institution spans 600+ acres in Kutch. He dedicates time to “Gau Seva”.

Devotional Patron

Mr. Sangoi is a spiritual leader and President of Shree KVO Jain Sthanak Dahisar (East). He also founded Kutch Jain Foundation – Mumbai and is on the committee of JITO, Bhayander Chapter.

Tribal School & Development

Education is empowerment. Under Mr. Sangoi’s leadership, Ramdev Group supports schools for Adivasi and Tribal children in Kutch. His commitment to community development is unwavering.

Medical Welfare

Medical support is vital to society. Mr. Sangoi is trustee of Ramdev Sevashram, Bhuj-Kutch, Manav Kalyan Kendra and Navneet Hi-tech Hospital Dahisar. His focus: healthcare for all.
